# H-1B Change of Employer

An employer may file an H-1B petition requesting a change of employer
for a qualifying worker.

Important factors may include:

- The person's present immigration circumstances
- Prior H-1B approval notices
- Form I-94 validity
- The timing and acceptance of the new petition
- Whether portability rules apply
- The proposed employment start date

A person should not assume that employment may begin merely because
a petition is being prepared or has been mailed.
