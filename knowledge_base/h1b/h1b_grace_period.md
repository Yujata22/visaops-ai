# H-1B Grace Period

Certain nonimmigrant workers may be eligible for a discretionary grace
period of up to 60 consecutive calendar days following the termination
of employment.

The grace period cannot extend beyond the expiration of the person's
currently authorized stay.

Potential options may include:

- Filing a change-of-employer petition
- Filing an application to change status
- Filing an eligible adjustment-related application
- Departing the United States

The availability and timing of these options depend on the person's
documents, filing history, authorized stay, and individual circumstances.

## Important considerations

- Last date of employment
- Form I-94 expiration date
- Petition validity period
- Filing and receipt dates
- Employment authorization rules
