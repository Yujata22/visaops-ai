# Change of Status

A change-of-status application requests a change from one nonimmigrant
classification to another while the applicant is in the United States.

Relevant considerations may include:

- Timely and proper filing
- Maintenance of the previous status
- Form I-94 expiration
- Eligibility for the requested classification
- USCIS approval
- Program start dates where F-1 status is requested

A pending change-of-status application does not automatically provide
employment authorization.
